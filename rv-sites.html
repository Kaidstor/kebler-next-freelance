RV Sites – Full Hook-Up and Electric Only

We offer full hook-up and electric only RV sites to our guests who like to travel in their second home! Guests will have access to their own fire pit and picnic table, guest WiFi, community bathrooms with showers, covered pavilion, and views of Anthracite Creek. Pet friendly! A 6 person maximum applies per site.

RV Sites – Full Hook-Up and Electric Only

The Marcellina Cabin — the most luxurious log cabin at Kebler Corner. Perfect for families or friends, it features a full kitchen with bar, spacious living room, washer & dryer, and a private deck with mountain views.

Cancellation Policy

  • Please call or email us if you need to cancel a reservation.
  • Cancelation Fee – A cancelation fee of $15 will be applied to any reservation that is canceled.
  • RV and Tent sites – 3+ day advanced notice is required for a full refund (minus service fee) or forfeit the first-night deposit.
  • Cabins – 10+ days advanced notice is required for a full refund (minus service fee) or forfeit the first-night deposit.
  • There are no refunds given for early departure.
